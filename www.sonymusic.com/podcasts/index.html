About Us

We are the Global Podcast Division of Sony Music Entertainment, based in New York, Los Angeles and London. Through our creator-first philosophy and diverse international partnerships we produce premium, must-listen shows that engage, entertain and delight podcast-lovers everywhere. Our dynamic creative, sales, marketing and business development teams ensure we offer a full service, multi-territory approach to modern podcast production.
